Xmas Dash is a gripping platform game that mixes rhythmic gameplay with festive Christmas vibes. Packed with colorful visuals and precise timing challenges, this platformer invites you to guide a lively icon through snowy tracks and flashing lights. Whether you're a seasoned jumper or a rhythm rookie, this game brings holiday magic to every tap. Get ready to sync your jumps and feel the beat!

DASH THROUGH TRAPS IN A MUSIC-BASED JOURNEY

Xmas Dash puts you in control of a speedy character racing across platforms filled with wintery hazards. From spiked candy canes to icy gaps, the route presents various traps that move in time with the soundtrack. Your objective is to precisely click to leap over obstacles and cross the finish line in a single flawless run. Be aware! Just one mistimed move will restart your journey, keeping the thrill alive.

Celebrate the Holidays with Style

What makes this platform game stand out is its holiday charm. This game includes seven Christmas-themed skins, letting you run as a cheerful snowman, a jolly Santa, or other festive characters. Besides, the snowy backgrounds, blinking decorations, and joyful music wrap each level in the Christmas spirit.

Guide Your Character

Just click or press the spacebar to jump. You sync every move to the music, turning your actions into a thrilling rhythm game.
